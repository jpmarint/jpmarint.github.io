CEDIA Experimental Aircraft
Design and construction of an airplane with the CEDIA group with the ability to fly from a single propeller, folding wings for minimum space needed, internal combustion and basic avionics elements. It is designed to carry a person.
Electric Generator Plates
It was created with the purpose of converting any highly traveled street or corridor into a source of electrical energy, by converting the passing of people or vehicles into electricity. The picture above was a design to transform the bridge between Haiti and Dominican Republic into an energy source where thousands of people cross daily.
Drone Controlled by Arm
Since many people don't know how to use a drone controller skillfully, we converted the controller to a "myo" band where your hand and its movements control the drone, making this interaction a bit more organic.
Electric Go-kart
For the "Electraton" competition in Mexico, we built an electric cart to the competition's specifications, which would run on rechargeable batteries on a track with low irregularities. Autonomy of 45 mins, capacity of up to 140 kg of load, 1-wheel drive, manual steering, cooling for greater efficiency, sensing of electrical variables and route mapping.
Baja SAE Car
Continuing the group's tradition, the goal was to build a new model of the all-terrain car for the "Baja SAE" competition in the United States. This car is internal combustion, two wheel drive, high performance suspension, disc brakes, speed changes, speed up to 60 km/h and 360° protection for the pilot.
Neural Network Cardboard Counter
Continuation of research for INOVACOR on the development of Artificial Intelligence for high-speed counting of cardboard sheets by means of computer vision.
Switched Source
Development of a switching power supply from scratch, using discrete control techniques for the creation of a controller. In addition to the development of the electronic system using microcontrollers and power systems, together with the creation of a reliable simulable system in SimuLink.
Robotic arm for Desktop
Based on the u-arm robotic arm and a stacker arm, I built this device from scratch mechanically and electronically; so that from a computer vision system that worked on raspberry and with a voice command system, it could take an object in its field of vision that you had requested.
Robotic Arm ABB - Cartoon Drawing Path
Development of a plugin for ABB's RobotStudio where you passed it an image of a cartoon or a drawing with only black lines, it detected them and then created a path to draw it on the board and delete it if desired.
F1 in Schools model
Development of a competition model car for F1 in Schools that was powered by a small compressed air tank and the goal was to reach the finish line as quickly as possible through the aerodynamic properties of the small vehicle.
Steering Wheel for electric go-kart
For the electric vehicle, the aim was to create an all-in-one system that would allow the vehicle to be monitored and controlled as much as possible during the competition and, in addition to establishing a communication line between the pits and the driver, where information about the car and a call.
Myers-Briggs Personality Type
The purpose of this project is to help the human resources department of Comfenalco at the moment of the hiring process to predict what kind types of personalities on the Myers Briggs a person will present based on the social text posts and comments.
Smart Home Model
Development of a model smart home system, to control lights, sound, and monitor the common areas of the house.
Inner Structure Concept for Aircraft Wings
Creating a hexagon-based honeycomb system for the internal structure of an aircraft wing and testing its elasticity, the concept was manufactured using 3D printing.
Ambulance "Drone" Concept
Ideation of a concept of a Drone that could load a medical team from an ambulance, so that it could attend emergency situations that a current car would take a long time. The drone could do it in minutes and agilely take the air path, as well as land on difficult terrain or on top of buildings or mountains.
